  <h1>Modern Data Warehouse for Business Intelligence ðŸš€</h1>
  <p>This project demonstrates a full-scale, end-to-end <strong>data warehousing solution</strong> that consolidates multiple disparate data sources into a single source of truth. It solves a real business problem: enabling companies to derive actionable insights for sales, marketing, and product optimization.</p>

  <div class="highlight">
    <p><strong>Problem Solved:</strong> Businesses struggle with fragmented ERP and CRM data, causing delays in decision-making and inaccurate reporting. This data warehouse centralizes data, enforces quality, and provides actionable insights.</p>
  </div>

  <h2>Key Features</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Scalable Medallion Architecture:</strong> Bronze, Silver, Gold layers ensure data quality, governance, and traceability.</li>
    <li><strong>Optimized Star Schema Modeling:</strong> Fact and dimension tables designed for high-performance queries.</li>
    <li><strong>Automated ETL Pipelines:</strong> SQL-based ETL scripts using <strong>Apache Airflow</strong>.</li>
    <li><strong>Actionable Business Insights:</strong> Dashboards and analytical models for customer, sales, and product performance.</li>
  </ul>
